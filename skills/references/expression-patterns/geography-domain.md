## Domain-Specific  module is designed to be read on its own.

| Scenario | Expression | 中文说明 |
|----------|------------|----------|
| Spatial framing | `The spatial distribution of [phenomenon] reveals substantial variation across [area].` | 用于空间格局描述。 |
| Urban morphology | `This pattern is associated with differences in urban form, land-use intensity, and street configuration.` | 适合讨论建成环境因素。 |
| Policy implication | `These findings provide actionable evidence for planners seeking to [goal].` | 强调规划与政策启示。 |
| Scale sensitivity | `The relationship varies across spatial scales, indicating that [interpretation].` | 适合多尺度分析。 |
| Place-based interpretation | `This contrast reflects the place-specific interaction between [factor] and [factor].` | 避免把不同城市差异简单归为噪声。 |

## Recommended Expressions

| Scenario | Expression | 中文说明 |
|----------|------------|----------|
| Study area sentence | `Located in [region], [city / district] offers a representative setting for examining [issue].` | 研究区介绍常用句。 |
| GIS evidence | `Spatial analysis based on [method] identifies clusters of [phenomenon].` | GIS 或空间统计分析描述。 |
| Planning implication | `From a planning perspective, the results highlight the need to [action].` | 连接结果与规划措施。 |
| Cross-city comparison | `The contrast between [city A] and [city B] underscores the role of [factor].` | 城市间对比分析。 |

## Avoid Expressions

| Avoid | Better Direction | 中文说明 |
|-------|------------------|----------|
| `The map clearly shows everything.` | State the specific spatial pattern | 地图解释不要写成泛泛结论。 |
| `Urban problems are becoming more serious.` | Specify the process, place, and evidence | 避免宏大空泛判断。 |
| `Policy makers should pay attention.` | State what action the evidence supports | 政策含义要落到可执行方向。 |
| `Different cities have different characteristics.` | Explain the relevant dimension of difference | 避免同义反复。 |

## Usage Scenarios

- study area、data description、discussion 和 conclusion 都可直接调用本模块。
- 当目标期刊强调地理空间视角时，可与 `ceus.md` 一起加载。
- 用于把纯方法或模型结果翻译成城市规划和空间分析语言。
- 用于跨城市、跨尺度、跨社区比较时的表达统一。

## Bilingual Example  module is designed to be read on its own.

| 中文意图 | English Pattern | Example |
|----------|-----------------|---------|
| 描述空间分布特征 | `The spatial distribution of [phenomenon] reveals substantial variation across [area].` | `The spatial distribution of perceived walkability reveals substantial variation across central districts and newly developed suburban communities.` |
| 提出规划含义 | `From a planning perspective, the results highlight the need to [action].` | `From a planning perspective, the results highlight the need to prioritize pedestrian comfort in transit-oriented renewal areas.` |
