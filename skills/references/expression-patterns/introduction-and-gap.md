# Introduction and Gap  module is designed to be read on its own.

 module is designed to be read on its own.

## Recommended Expressions

| Scenario | Expression | 中文说明 |
|----------|------------|----------|
| Background importance | `[Topic] has become increasingly important in [field].` | 用于稳健引出研究背景，避免空泛开场。 |
| Real-world motivation | `Understanding [phenomenon] is essential for [decision / policy / planning task].` | 强调研究问题与实际应用的关系。 |
| Literature transition | `Recent studies have shown growing interest in [topic], especially in relation to [subtopic].` | 从背景转入文献回顾时使用。 |
| Gap statement | `However, existing studies remain limited in their ability to [specific limitation].` | 用于指出具体不足，优先写可验证限制。 |
| Contribution bridge | `To address this limitation, this study develops [method / framework / dataset].` | 从问题顺畅转入本文贡献。 |

## Avoid Expressions

| Avoid | Better Direction | 中文说明 |
|-------|------------------|----------|
| `Nowadays, ...` | Start with the topic or phenomenon directly | 学术引言里避免口语化时间状语开场。 |
| `There are many studies about ...` | Name the research stream or finding | 不要用空泛文献总述。 |
| `This paper is very innovative ...` | State the concrete contribution | 避免自夸，改写成可观察贡献。 |
| `Few people have studied ...` | Specify what is missing in design, data, scale, or theory | 不要用模糊的“很少有人研究”。 |

## Usage Scenarios

- 写摘要前两句时，用于说明研究问题的重要性与研究空白。
- 写引言第一段时，用于连接现实问题、政策背景和研究主题。
- 写文献综述过渡句时，用于把已有成果和本文定位清楚分开。
- 写 contribution 段时，用于从 gap 过渡到 method / data / evidence。
